Frequently Asked Questions

Who should attend the Work Without Limits™ Virtual Summit?

Executive leaders, hiring managers, top independent professionals, agencies, influencers, and academics who are interested in learning how to unlock their growth potential in the new world of work.

How do I register for the Work Without Limits™ Virtual Summit?

You will find the registration form above and at the bottom of the page. Once you fill in the registration form, you will receive more information on the event and how to join on August 25.

Is there a charge to register and attend the Work Without Limits™ Virtual Summit?

There is no charge to attend the virtual summit—it is free to all attendees this year.

Can I invite my colleagues or friends to attend?

The summit is open to all business leaders, select independent professionals, and agencies who want to learn how they can drive better business outcomes by embracing innovative work models.

What are the dates and times of Work Without Limits™ Virtual Summit?

Work Without Limits™ Virtual Summit is taking place on Wednesday, August 25, 2021, from 11:00am – 3:00pm ET | 8:00am – 12:00pm PT

If I can’t attend a session, can I watch the session recordings afterward?

Yes! While we encourage viewers to join live to access the full event, many sessions will be recorded. Those who register will have on-demand access to the recordings following the conclusion of the event on August 25.

Where can I find the lineup of keynote and session speakers?

Check back soon for speaker announcements.

  • Firefox versions >= 76

Where do I stay current on Work Without Limits™ developments?

We encourage you to watch this space for further updates on the event. After you register, you will receive emails with new developments as well as get access to the event platform to review the agenda.

Is there a mobile app?

No, there is not a mobile app; however, you do have the ability to watch the summit from your mobile device with the unique summit link you will receive after registration. We would recommend viewing on a larger device for better functionality and overall experience.

We will continue to evaluate the best format and experience to serve our global community as we plan future events.
